The Complete AI Claims Pipeline

Every step from document upload to settlement recommendation is handled automatically.

🔍
Smart OCR
Tesseract OCR extracts text from images and scanned PDFs with confidence scoring and auto-retry.
🤖
Gemini AI Extraction
Gemini 2.5 Flash intelligently extracts structured fields from 7+ document types with zero hallucination.
🚨
Fraud Detection
Multi-signal fraud analysis covering name mismatches, format violations, date inconsistencies, and more.
✅
Policy Verification
Cross-checks claim data against MongoDB policy records for Aadhaar, PAN, and claim type classification.
💰
Claim Estimation
Instant payout estimation based on claim type, sum assured, and double indemnity rules.
📄
PDF Reports
Auto-generated professional claim settlement reports with full audit trail, downloadable as PDF.
